# Proposed Features for ZealQuiz

To enhance the exam preparation experience for your mom, here are the proposed features:

### 1. Interactive Study Features
- **Live Score Tracker**: A real-time counter (e.g., `Correct: 12 / 50`) that updates as she clicks options.
- **Visual Progress Bar**: A green bar at the top of the screen to show how many questions she has left.
- **Completion Report Card**: A summary at the end showing her total score and a "Retake" button to try the quiz again.

### 2. Quiz Management
- **Search Bar in History**: Easily find past quizzes by typing keywords (e.g., "Tamil", "Model Test 16").
- **Delete History**: Ability to remove old quizzes to keep the list clean.
- **Auto-Save Progress**: Even if she closes the tab mid-quiz, she can come back and see where she left off (using browser local storage).

### 3. Professional PDF Printing
- **Custom PDF Header**: A field to add a "Name" and "Date" at the top of the PDF so it looks like a real exam paper.
- **Correct Answer Key**: In addition to marking answers on the questions, we can add a summary "Answer Key" at the very end of the PDF.
